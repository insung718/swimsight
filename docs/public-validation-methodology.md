# Public Validation Methodology

`/validation` is an honesty surface, not a marketing accuracy claim.

## Production status

The page reports `UNTRAINED` while production uses the deterministic conservative ensemble. A learned champion can appear only when a non-deterministic registry entry passes governance gates, references a sealed immutable cohort version, and meets configured sample requirements.

## Public eligibility

- Current public-research consent is active.
- Minors also have verified guardian consent.
- Evaluated outcomes come from research-grade imported official individual results with row provenance, original-row hash, external meet ID, and external result ID.
- Predictions existed before the matched result and match account, event, course, and exact target date.

Self-declared official results remain available privately but do not support public performance claims.

## Suppression

Eligible athlete/race counts are hidden below 25 athletes. MAE, median absolute error, interval coverage, baseline comparisons, Brier score, and calibration error appear only after at least 30 evaluated predictions across at least 25 athletes. Subgroup metrics remain unpublished until their own privacy and stability thresholds are met.

## Baselines

SwimSight is compared with last-race, last-three-average, linear-trend, and conservative deterministic baselines using only rows where the corresponding baseline and official outcome are both available. Every metric reports its usable sample rather than inheriting a larger headline count.

## Limitations

The current accounts may not represent competitive swimmers by age, category, region, event, or course. Exact historical age is unavailable without date of birth. Imported data can contain source errors. Forecast association does not establish causal training effects, medical readiness, or safe training advice.

## Claims that require real longitudinal evidence

- Accuracy or calibration for the swimmer population.
- Reliable improvement over every baseline.
- Subgroup fairness or absence of material regression.
- Generalization to elite, very young, sparse-history, or underrepresented swimmers.
- Causal benefit from any training recommendation.
- Stable 7/30-day retention or pilot conversion.
